## üìù Step-by-Step Recording Process

### BEFORE RECORDING:

**1. Prepare Your Browser**
- Open Chrome in **Incognito mode** (no extensions, clean)
- Zoom to 100% (Ctrl + 0)
- Hide bookmarks bar (Ctrl + Shift + B)
- Set resolution to **1920x1080** (for 16:9) or record phone in **1080x1920** (for 9:16)

**2. Enlarge Cursor**
- Windows: Settings ‚Üí Accessibility ‚Üí Mouse Pointer ‚Üí Size = **6-7**
- Choose **White with Black Outline** or **Black with White Outline**
- Enable "Mouse Pointer Trails" (optional for emphasis)

**3. Prepare Demo Content**
- Have a REAL fridge photo ready (your own or stock image)
- Practice the flow 2-3 times before recording
- Clear cookies/cache for fresh demo

**4. OBS Setup for 1:1 (Instagram/LinkedIn Square)**
- Open OBS Studio
- Settings ‚Üí Video ‚Üí Base Canvas: **1080x1080**
- Settings ‚Üí Video ‚Üí Output: **1080x1080**
- Add Source: "Display Capture" or "Window Capture" (Chrome only)
- Center your browser window in frame

**5. OBS Setup for 9:16 (Reels/TikTok Vertical)**
- Settings ‚Üí Video ‚Üí Base Canvas: **1080x1920**
- Record your phone screen using **ApowerREC** or **AZ Screen Recorder**
- OR use mobile browser on desktop (Chrome DevTools F12 ‚Üí Toggle Device)

---

### DURING RECORDING:

**Recording Settings (OBS):**
1. Click "Settings" ‚Üí "Output"
2. Recording Quality: **High Quality, Medium File Size**
3. Recording Format: **MP4**
4. Encoder: **Hardware (NVENC)** if you have NVIDIA GPU, else **x264**
5. Click "Start Recording"

**Cursor Movement Tips:**
- Move cursor SLOWLY and DELIBERATELY
- Pause 1-2 seconds before each click
- Circle important elements (move cursor in circle around text/button)
- Use smooth, curved movements (not jerky straight lines)
- Hover over buttons for 1 second before clicking

**Timing:**
- Each action should take **3-5 seconds**
- Add **2-second pauses** between major steps
- Total raw footage should be **90-120 seconds** (will edit down to 60)

---

### AFTER RECORDING:

**1. Import to CapCut**
- Open CapCut Desktop/Mobile
- Import your OBS recording
- Drag to timeline

**2. Edit for Each Platform**

#### For Instagram Reels / TikTok (9:16):
```
Timeline:
0:00 - Hook (text: "What's for dinner?")
0:05 - Problem (text: "Stop wasting food!")
0:10 - Solution (text: "fridgeo.app = FREE AI recipes")
0:20 - Demo Part 1: Photo capture
0:35 - Demo Part 2: AI detection
0:45 - Demo Part 3: Recipe generation
0:55 - CTA (text: "Link in bio! Try FREE")
```

**CapCut Edits:**
- **Auto Captions:** Click "Text" ‚Üí "Auto Captions" (generates from audio)
- **Text Overlays:** Add animated text at each scene
  - Font: Bold, Sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat, Poppins)
  - Size: Large (60-80pt)
  - Color: White with black outline OR brand color #92d5c6
  - Animation: "Pop" or "Typewriter" effect
- **Speed:** Speed up boring parts (1.2x-1.5x)
- **Transitions:** "Smooth Slide" between scenes
- **Music:** Add trending background music (search "tech vibe" or "upbeat cooking")
  - Volume: 20-30% (don't overpower)
- **Sound Effects:** Click sounds when tapping buttons

**3. Add Cursor Effects (Optional)**
- In CapCut: "Effects" ‚Üí "Mouse Click Effect"
- Add yellow glow around cursor on important clicks

**4. Export Settings**

## üé• PRO TIPS

### Make Your Cursor REALLY Stand Out:
1. **Windows Accessibility:**
   - Settings ‚Üí Accessibility ‚Üí Mouse Pointer
   - Choose **"White"** or **"Inverted"** (high contrast)
   - Size: **7/7** (largest)
   - Enable pointer trails for motion emphasis

2. **Use Cursor Effects in Post:**
   - CapCut: Add yellow/green glow around cursor
   - Zoom in slightly on cursor when clicking important buttons

3. **Click Animations:**
   - Add "POP" or "BURST" effect on each click
   - Use yellow circle ripple effect

### Speed Up Editing:
- Record at 1920x1080, export multiple aspect ratios:
  - **9:16** for Reels/TikTok (crop sides)
  - **1:1** for Instagram/Facebook/LinkedIn feed (crop top/bottom)
  - **16:9** for YouTube/Twitter (full frame)

### Engagement Hacks:
- **Pattern Interrupt:** Start with unexpected visual (e.g., fridge full of random stuff)
- **Text Hooks:** "Wait for it..." or "You won't believe this..."
- **Trending Audio:** Search "trending sounds" on TikTok/Reels daily
- **Call-out Comments:** Pin "What's in YOUR fridge? Comment below! üëá"

### Analytics to Track:
- **3-second view rate** (did they watch past hook?)
- **Average watch time** (aim for 50%+)
- **Shares** (most important metric for growth)
- **Link clicks** (track in Netlify analytics)
